About Wonder+ Pipes

Quality-focused manufacturer of uPVC, SN8 and perforated pipes for plumbing, irrigation and infrastructure projects. ISO 9001:2015 certified with a dedicated testing lab and supply chain for project deliveries.

Who we are

Wonder+ Pipes is an experienced piping manufacturer headquartered in Jaipur. We supply municipal, highway and commercial projects with ISI and ISO certified piping solutions and active technical support.

Our capabilities

  • Monthly capacity ~200 MT with phased delivery coordination
  • ISI 4985 / IS 15328 compliance testing & certifications
  • In-house lab for tensile, hydrostatic and dimensional tests
  • Turnkey logistics for large projects
